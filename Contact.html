What is the difference between the watches that have only gps and the ones with gps+cellular ?

There's some well-meaning but incorrect info in the answers so hopefully this is more clear: GPS+Cellular models do not "have to" have cellular service, you can choose to buy that model for later cellular use but still use the Apple Watch in the meantime without cellular for all its other functions.

Can I pair this watch with iPhone ?

Yes, but to put it simply; Apple and Android in general don't normally get along. While Apple has contracted Samsung to make screens for the iPhone, you're still going to get best performance with an Apple Watch and an iPhone or a Samsung (or better) and this smart watch.

Do I need to have a separate data plan
or add this as a device on my wireless phone plan in order to use or to make calls ?

The Bluetooth version does not require a data plan as it communicates through your phone. LTE version requires a data plan to use as a stand alone.

Does it track your sleep, light, rem, deep, awake ?

Sure it does. After the watch is connected with your phone, you can check your light/deep sleep and awake hours via our official app "Veryfitpro".
